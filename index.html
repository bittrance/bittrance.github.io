➔ Truncating a Kafka topic

Published: Friday, Apr 2, 2021   |   3 minute read
Sometimes, you want to get rid of messages from a Kafka topic. Perhaps you have consumers that always start from the beginning, or you realize the whole topic is full of messages with sensitive data that must not reach consumers. In theory, you can easily delete a Kafka topic. However, this is quite disruptive and is likely to fail both producers and consumers on this topic even if you quickly recreate the topic.
